Chess is a game that has been studied for centuries. Many developments and iterations have been made to the game to create the current rendition that is played today. Ancient versions of the game didn't even have check or checkmate (you did not need to notice your opponent if their king was under attack) this mean't the king could be taken like any other piece on the board, except taking the king ended the game
